## 🎯 **BEST PRACTICES**

### **Personalization Tips**
- Research company before outreach
- Reference specific challenges or achievements
- Use industry-specific terminology
- Mention mutual connections or interests
- Customize benefits for their business type

### **Timing Strategies**
- Tuesday-Thursday: Best response rates
- 10 AM - 2 PM: Peak engagement times
- Follow-up every 3-4 days
- Respect time zones for international prospects

### **Subject Line Optimization**
- Use emojis for visual appeal
- Include personalization tokens
- Create urgency without being spammy
- Test different length variations
- Include industry-specific keywords
