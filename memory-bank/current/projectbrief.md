# Project Brief - AgentFlow Pro

## Infrastruktura

- **Database:** Supabase (PostgreSQL) – DATABASE_URL v `.env.local`
- Glej [docs/database-setup.md](../../docs/database-setup.md) za nastavitev

## Vision

Multi-Agent AI Platform ki omogoča business avtomatizacijo z orkestracijo več AI agentov.

## Core Value Proposition

- 10x hitrejša business avtomatizacija
- Multi-agent coordination brez kodiranja
- Knowledge graph za long-term context
- Pay-per-use monetizacija

## Target Market (primary)

- Hotels & Resorts ($59/mesec)
- DMOs & Destination Marketing ($99/mesec)
- Tour Operators & Travel Agencies ($99–499/mesec)

## Positioning

AI Content Platform specializiran za turizem – destination guides, hotel copy, seasonal campaigns, SEO za direktne rezervacije.

## Success Criteria

- MVP v 7 dneh
- First customer v 30 dneh
- $1,000 MRR v 60 dneh
- $10,000 MRR v 180 dneh
