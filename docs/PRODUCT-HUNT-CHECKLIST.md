# Product Hunt Launch Checklist

Based on [product-hunt-launch-strategy.md](../product-hunt-launch-strategy.md).

## Pre-Launch (2 weeks before)

### Week 1: Assets

- [ ] **Gallery images** (1200x800px)
  - Hero with branding
  - Dashboard screenshot
  - Workflow visualization
  - Tourism use case
  - Testimonial graphic
- [ ] **Demo video** (30–60 sec)
  - Problem → Solution → CTA
- [ ] **Product Hunt description** (tagline + description)
- [ ] **Hunter outreach** – identify 20 hunters, send preview

### Week 2: Community

- [ ] Comment on relevant Product Hunt launches
- [ ] Follow hunters and makers in AI/tourism/SaaS
- [ ] Prepare social posts (LinkedIn, Twitter, etc.)
- [ ] Email waitlist ready

## Launch Day

- [ ] 9:00 AM – Product Hunt live
- [ ] 9:05 AM – Maker comment
- [ ] 9:10 AM – LinkedIn
- [ ] 9:15 AM – Twitter thread
- [ ] 9:20 AM – Instagram
- [ ] 10:00 AM – Email to waitlist
- [ ] Monitor comments, respond within 1 hour
- [ ] Thank voters

## Post-Launch

- [ ] Thank-you post
- [ ] Follow up with signups
- [ ] Gather testimonials from new users

## Assets Folder

Create `public/product-hunt/` and add:

- `hero.png` (1200x800)
- `dashboard.png`
- `workflow.png`
- `demo.mp4` (or link)
