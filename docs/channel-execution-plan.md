## 🚨 **RISK MITIGATION**

### **Channel-Specific Risks**
- **Direct Outreach**: Low response rates, email deliverability issues
- **LinkedIn Groups**: Low engagement, algorithm changes affecting reach
- **Tourism Associations**: Long partnership cycles, bureaucratic delays
- **Personal Network**: Limited scale, dependency on individual relationships

### **Mitigation Strategies**
- **Multi-Channel Approach**: Diversify acquisition sources
- **Rapid Testing**: Quick iteration on messaging and offers
- **Fallback Plans**: Alternative channels ready if primary underperform
- **Resource Flexibility**: Ability to reallocate budget and personnel
- **Performance Monitoring**: Real-time tracking and optimization
